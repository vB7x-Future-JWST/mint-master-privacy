Privacy Policy · Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Mint Master ("the Bot", "we", "our") is a turn-based PVP strategy game application for Discord, featuring deck battles, collectible cards, and competitive gameplay. The Bot is developed and operated by Wild Hare ("the Developer").

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Bot collects, why it is collected, how it is stored and protected, and what rights you have over your data. By adding the Bot to a Discord server or interacting with it, you agree to the practices described in this policy. The Bot operates in accordance with Discord's Developer Terms of Service and Developer Policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot collects and processes only the information necessary to provide its game and server-protection features:

  • Discord account identifiers. Your Discord user ID, username, and avatar, as provided publicly through the Discord API. We never have access to your email address, password, or payment details.
  • Server identifiers. Server (guild) IDs, channel IDs, and role IDs required for the Bot to operate its commands, rewards, and logging in the correct places.
  • Message content (processed, not stored). In servers where it is enabled, the Bot reads message content for two purposes only: (a) responding to game commands, and (b) automated security scanning that detects scam, phishing, and malicious links. Message content is analyzed in real time and is not saved to our database. If a message is flagged by the security system, a record of that message (its text, author ID, and timestamp) may be posted to a private moderation channel inside the same Discord server so that server staff can review the incident.
  • Voice activity metadata. Voice channel join and leave events and session durations, used solely to grant in-game activity rewards. The Bot never records, stores, or listens to audio.
  • Gameplay data. Card collections, token balances, pack-opening history, achievements, PVP battle results, cooldown timestamps, and leaderboard statistics associated with your Discord user ID.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide core game functionality: card packs, decks, PVP battles, achievements, and progression.
  • To grant activity rewards based on text and voice participation.
  • To display leaderboards, battle logs, and other in-game statistics within Discord.
  • To protect servers through automated detection of scam and phishing links.
  • To prevent cheating, abuse, and exploitation of game systems.
  • To comply with Discord's Developer Terms of Service and Developer Policy.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Bot's stated features.

4. Data Storage and Security

Collected data is stored in a secured MongoDB database. Access to the database is restricted to the Developer and protected by authentication credentials. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

Gameplay and account data are retained for as long as you use the Bot, so that your progress, collection, and rewards are preserved. Data belonging to users who request deletion is removed permanently. Inactive or obsolete records may be periodically purged at the Developer's discretion.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party. Your information is not shared outside the Bot's systems, with two exceptions:

  • Within Discord servers: gameplay information (such as leaderboard rankings, battle results, and reward announcements) and security incident logs are visible to members or moderators of the server in which they occur, as part of normal Bot operation.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information if required to do so by law or a valid legal request.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time request access to the data the Bot holds about you, or request its permanent deletion, by contacting the Developer using the details in Section 10. Please note that deleting your data will permanently erase your game progress, card collection, and token balance, and this cannot be undone.

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is intended only for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirement (13 years old, or higher where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below this age. If we learn that such data has been collected, it will be deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
